FLUORINE

PRONUNCIATION: Fluor-ine

Fluorine is the first element and the lightest in the family of halogen gases. It is the most chemically reactive element and is extremely dangerous and poisonous.

Fluorine is used in rocket fuel, because it is a very reactive element it makes it good for explosive reactions. Fluorine can also be found in toothpaste. Scientists put very small amounts of fluorine inside toothpaste to help make your teeth whiter.

APPEARANCE Pale yellow gas with a strong odor
DISCOVERY DATE 1886
DISCOVERED BY Henri Moissan
