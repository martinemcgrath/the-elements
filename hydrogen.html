HYDROGEN

Hydrogren is the first element in the periodic table and is the lightest and most common of all elements in the universe. It is an essential element of our world. Hydrogen reacts explosively when in contact with the elements oxygen, chlorine and fluorine.

Hydrogren can be found in the sun. It is very easy to burn, one of the reasons why the sun burns so brightly.

APPEARANCE Colourless, odourless gas
DISCOVERY DATE 1766
DISCOVERED BY Henry Cavendish
ATOMIC NUMBER 1
